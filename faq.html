If the amount due for a bill changes from month to month, what should I do?

If the amount due for a bill changes from month to month, what should I do?

We suggest using the upper limit for this bill as the amount. This way, you will have the money for the bill, no matter what the monthly charge is. The remainder will carry over for the next time it is due.

How do I add expenses?

How do I add expenses?

Select the plus sign next to the envelope you want to add an expense to. Here, you can transfer from other envelopes to the selected envelope as well.

Can I delete/edit a transaction?

Can I delete/edit a transaction?

No. But if you entered a transaction by mistake, you can adjust your bank balance. If you adjust up, it will put the extra money into unaccounted, which can then be moved back to your envelope. If you adjust down, and your unaccounted was set to 0, you will have a negative unaccounted. Remove money from an envelope to bring the unaccounted back to 0 or positive.

Why is unaccounted yellow?

Why is unaccounted yellow?

One goal of this app is to have all your money accounted for. You want your unaccounted amount to be at 0.
